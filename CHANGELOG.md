# 🚀 Release Notes - v2.2 (UI & Experience Update)

## ✨ What’s New & Visual Redesign (Material Design 3)
 
- **Complete Interface Overhaul:**  
  We have redesigned the entire application following Google’s modern standards (Material Design 3). Say goodbye to heavy shadows and hello to a much cleaner, professional, and streamlined look!

- **Modern Cards & Buttons:**  
  We implemented **Outlined Cards** and **Tonal Buttons** on key screens such as *My Agenda*, *My Results*, and *Book a Consultation*, significantly improving readability and focus.

- **Refreshed Sidebar:**  
  Featuring a new rounded **pill-style selection design** for smoother and more intuitive navigation.

- **Unified Date Formatting:**  
  We standardized all dates across the app (filters and cards) to the **DD-MM-YY** hyphenated format to eliminate visual confusion and maintain consistency.

---

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Performance Improvements

- **Precise Results Filtering:**  
  Resolved an issue where the *"Study Type"* filter was not applying correctly. Search queries are now executed using the exact analysis identifier, guaranteeing accurate results.

- **Account Deletion Security:**  
  Enhanced validation on the *"My Profile"* screen. The **"Delete Account"** button now only activates after typing the word **"ELIMINAR"** exactly in uppercase, preventing accidental data loss.
